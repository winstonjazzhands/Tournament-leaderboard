Every week, DFK players step into the arena and compete for tournament glory — and the more 1st place you stack at Level 10 and Level 20, the bigger your payout.

Prizes are distributed weekly in JEWEL every Sunday/Monday, based on your total win count.

🏆 Level 10 Rewards

  • 3 wins = 150 JEWEL
  • 5 wins = 300 JEWEL
  • 10 wins = 750 JEWEL

⚔️ Level 20 Rewards

  • Each win = 60 JEWEL

Consistent winners get rewarded. The more first-place you claim, the more JEWEL you take home.
